Who is Everlyne Kaberere?

Everlyne Njeri Kaberere is a young Kenyan lady born in 25th November 1995. She went through the Kenyan 844 education system and she graduated from Dedan Kimathi University of Technology (DeKUT) in 28th April 2017. Back in campus, she was the Google Women TechMaker (WTM), GDG Nyeri for a year. It is a community that provides visibilty, resources and a community to women in technology all over the world.

She is passionate about technology and she loves what she does and interacts with as far as technology is concerned. She would want to majorly focus on front-end development. She is a thinker, keen to detail and a good listener.

She is currently an Entrepreneur In Training (EIT) at Meltwater Entrepreneurial School of Technology (MEST) in Accra, Ghana.
